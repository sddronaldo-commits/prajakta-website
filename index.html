About

I am Prajakta Pharande, a Psychology postgraduate working at the intersection of psychology, user experience, and social impact. I use psychology and user research methods to understand behavior, participation, and user experiences in social impact contexts.

I am drawn to work that combines human understanding with meaningful systems change.

Rather than approaching digital work only through technology, I am interested in how products, processes, and experiences shape people's everyday realities — especially in spaces working toward social good.
