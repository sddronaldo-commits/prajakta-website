About

A Psychology postgraduate and UX researcher — I want to use design and behavioral insights to create meaningful social impact.

With a background spanning user research, UX design, organizational psychology, and mental health, I bring an interdisciplinary approach to understanding human behavior and translating insights into intuitive, empathy-driven, user-centered experiences.

I'm drawn to work that combines human understanding with meaningful systems change.

Rather than approaching digital work only through technology, I'm interested in how products, processes, and experiences shape people's everyday realities — especially in spaces working toward social good.
